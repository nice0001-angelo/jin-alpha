Michelle

소고기덮밥(30분)

  1. 가쓰오다시 1/2C : 물 1/2C+다시 넣고 끓으면 다시만 버리고 다시물을 공기에 담아서 가쓰오부시를 위에 넣어두고 8분간 우린다 그 후에 면포를 깐 채반위에 부어서 찌꺼기가 없는 맑은 가쓰오다시를 만든다.
  2. 양파, 팽이버섯, 실파 : 각각 4Cm.
  3. 소고기 : 결반대로 얇게 편썰기 1*5*0.2cm. - Tip: 편썰고 길이를 끝 잘라서 맞추기.
  4. 김 : 구운후(빳빳하면 안구워도 됨) 가늘게 채(하리노리).
  5. 달걀 풀어두기.
  6. 돈부리다시 : 가쓰오다시 1/2C + 간장1T + 맛술1T + 설탕 1t + 소금 약간.
  7. 오믈렛 프라이팬에 돈부리다시 넣고 - 끓으면 고기 -> 양파, 팽이 -> 실파 -> 풀어둔 계란을 전체적으로 뿌린다(80%정도까지만 익힘).
  8. 밥위에 7번에서 완성한 돈부리다시 소스를 올리고 - 김채 올려서 마무리.

    9. * 계란은 반숙이 되도록 해야 함.

    * 밥이 보이지 않도록 잘 덮어야 함. - 미리 밥모양이 잘 나오게 살짝 볼록하게 밥을 자리 잡아 놓는게 중요.

    요구사항


    가. 덮밥용 양념간장(돈부리 다시)을 만들어 사용하시오.
    나. 고기, 채소, 달걀은 재료 특성에 맞게 조리하여 준비한 밥 위에 올려 놓으시오.
    다. 김을 구워 칼로 잘게 썰어(하리노리) 사용하시오.

Inspiration from Mother

Michelle

I found a real passion in my career which is inspired by my mother and our home’s kitchen. My potential culinary capacity was built-up by the mother who taught me fundamental knowledge and transmitted valuable experiences to me through her cooking journey.

Jee

When it was my first time and not easy to take care of my baby, I got a lot of help from my mother. She always gave me courage, hope, and support. She is the owner-chef of a Japanese Restaurant in Korea. Furthermore, she is good at all Korean cuisine. Her strengths are thorough hygiene and consistent devotion. I started to help her job and I learned and realized a lot from serving, cashiers, kitchen assistants and even buying ingredients. This was a real-world of the restaurant, not a practice. Now and forever, I think the greatest thing in the world is the mother.

Our Future


We thought a lot about our life, cooking, our home countries, and Canada while starting this food blog. For example, South Korea offers free lunch to all public school students. Surprisingly, it is not low-quality meals and is also not cheap ingredients. Nutritionists manage daily diets carefully and use organic ingredients. There are even parent's monitors regularly. Most children love school lunch very much and to be honest, it is often tastier than moms. There are always fresh fruits and vegetables every weekday and there are birthday cake and ice cream on a special day. Of course, Canadian mothers didn’t believe this so far! They said Canadian consisted of various races; therefore, a nutritionist can’t cook according to the tastes of all students. Their prediction is very interesting. How can we apply the merits of our home countries here? How can Canadians live more comfortably? The answer to this will be our task in the future. Ultimately, we want to contribute to Canada in the field of cooking and baking by making our effort and more creative ideas.

As a first step to become a good chef, now we basically have to eat hard to distinguish between good taste and aroma. Let’s continue eating over and over!






Lovely Fat Man: Eating and Eating


Who is he?

JongWon Baek is known as a talented Korean chef, He is on the Entertainment Television program called Baek Jongwon's Alley Restaurant. It is a South Korean cooking-variety program that has been broadcast since January 5, 2018. It is a spin-off of the cooking-variety program Baek Jongwon's Top 3 Chef King. It is currently hosted by JongWon Baek and SeongJu Kim, it aired on SBS: Seoul Broadcasting System every Friday at 23:20 KST: Korea Standard Time. 

                                           

Why we choose him?

JongWon Baek grew up in a rich family, entered a top university in South Korea, and was always in a stable environment, but all he wanted, was not economic stability but making tasty food. For example, JongWon Baek, who graduated from the Department of Social Work (not Culinary) had a very interesting university life. Instead of studying, he traveled across the country in search of tasty restaurants, which helped him accumulate an extensive and comprehensive knowledge of food. He would often cook for his classmates and soon became a central figure in his department. JongWon Baek, enjoy eating and likes friendly, simple, but delicious rice and food more than expensive and complex luxury dishes. It is Korean homemade food. It is a home-made meal including a mother's finger touch and heart. It is the reason we choose him.

His value

In a Television program called Baek JongWon' Alley Restaurant, JongWon Baek visits a food alley where self-employed people struggle with that business has been slow and renews them from store interior to food and other business skills. With his help, many self-employed people have actually achieved great success in the food business. He is a savior of the South Korean diet. He is worth more than a cook.


What we will do?

We will use JongWon Baek's recipe and add creativity with fresh ingredients to create our unique dish.
